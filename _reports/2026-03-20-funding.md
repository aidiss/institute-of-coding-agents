## Acquisitions & Exits

### The Windsurf Saga (May–July 2025) — Confirmed
The most complex deal sequence of the year:

1. **OpenAI-Windsurf acquisition (~$3B, May 2025) — COLLAPSED:** OpenAI entered exclusivity for a $3B acquisition of Windsurf. Deal fell apart when Microsoft insisted on rights to Windsurf IP under its OpenAI partnership agreement; OpenAI refused, viewing Windsurf's tech as competitive against GitHub Copilot.
2. **Google acqui-hires Windsurf founders (Jul 11, 2025, ~$2.4B total):** Google paid ~$1.2B to investors and ~$1.2B in compensation packages to ~40 employees joining Google DeepMind, including co-founders Varun Mohan and Douglas Chen. Google received a non-exclusive technology license — not a full equity acquisition. Precedent set by earlier Character.AI deal.
3. **Cognition acquires remaining Windsurf entity (Jul 14, 2025):** Cognition acquired Windsurf's IP, product, trademark, brand, and remaining ~210 staff. Jeff Wang became interim CEO of the Windsurf unit. Combined ARR approximately doubled to ~$150M.

### Anthropic Acquires Bun (December 2025) — Confirmed
Anthropic's first-ever acquisition. Bun (JavaScript runtime by Jarred Sumner, 7M monthly downloads) acquired to own the execution layer underlying Claude Code. Terms undisclosed. Bun remains open-source (MIT license).

### Anthropic Acquires Vercept (2025) — Confirmed
Top computer vision team acquired as a strategic bet on agent visual perception capabilities. Terms undisclosed.

### Anysphere (Cursor) Acquisitions (2025) — Confirmed
Three acquisitions in rapid succession:
- **Supermaven (Nov 2024):** Founded by Jacob Jackson (ex-Tabnine, ex-OpenAI); 35,000 developers on platform; had raised $12M from Bessemer and John Schulman. Acquired to accelerate Cursor's Tab AI model for long-context, low-latency code completion.
- **Koala (~$129M, Jul 2025):** AI-powered CRM startup acqui-hired. Product shut down Sep 2025; select engineers joined to build enterprise capabilities.
- **Growth by Design (Nov 2025):** Tech recruiting strategy company. Terms undisclosed.
- **Graphite ("way over" $290M, Dec 2025):** Code review platform serving 500+ companies including Shopify, Snowflake, Figma, Perplexity. Had raised $52M Series B in Mar 2025 (Accel, Anthropic's Anthology Fund). Graphite continues operating independently.

### Poolside Acquires Fern Labs (November 2025) — Confirmed
Terms undisclosed. Fern Labs acquired to augment Poolside's enterprise AI coding model capabilities.

### OpenAI Acquires Convogo Team (January 2026) — Confirmed
Acqui-hire of the team behind executive coaching AI tool Convogo. Product wound down; team redirected to "AI cloud efforts." OpenAI's ninth acquisition in roughly one year.

### Character.AI / Noam Shazeer → Google (~$2.7B, August 2024) — Confirmed (Reference)
Google licensing/talent deal returned Noam Shazeer and Daniel De Freitas plus ~30 researchers to Google DeepMind to co-lead Gemini. Set structural precedent for the later Windsurf deal. DOJ and FTC opened investigations into whether such "pseudo-acquisitions" circumvent antitrust review.

## Talent Moves

### Google's "Acqui-hire" Strategy
- **Noam Shazeer + Daniel De Freitas** (Character.AI co-founders): Part of $2.7B deal (Aug 2024). Both now co-lead Gemini at Google DeepMind.
- **Varun Mohan + Douglas Chen + ~40 R&D staff** (Windsurf/Codeium): Part of $2.4B deal (Jul 2025). Joined Google DeepMind to advance agentic coding and Gemini integration.

### Thinking Machines Lab (Founded Feb 2025)
Mass departure of senior OpenAI talent to Mira Murati's new company:
- **Mira Murati** (former OpenAI CTO) — founder & CEO
- **John Schulman** (OpenAI co-founder, briefly at Anthropic) — co-founder
- **Barrett Zoph** (former OpenAI VP of Research) — co-founder
- **Lilian Weng** (former OpenAI Safety/Research leader) — co-founder
- **Andrew Tulloch, Luke Metz** (ex-OpenAI/Meta) — co-founders

### Anthropic
- **Jarred Sumner** (Bun founder): Joined Anthropic following Bun acquisition (Dec 2025). Bun project remains open-source.

### Meta
- **Yann LeCun**: Departed Meta (2026) to establish an independent world model research lab; reportedly pursuing a $5B valuation.

### Unconventional AI (Founded Dec 2025)
- **Naveen Rao** (former head of AI at Databricks, founder of MosaicML acquired by Databricks for $1.3B, founder of Nervana acquired by Intel for $400M+): Founded Unconventional AI. Raised $475M seed at $4.5B from a16z and Lightspeed.

### Microsoft
- **Mustafa Suleiman** (DeepMind co-founder): Microsoft paid $650M for Inflection AI (Mar 2024); Suleiman became CEO of Microsoft AI.

### Anysphere (Cursor)
- **Jacob Jackson** (Supermaven founder, ex-Tabnine, ex-OpenAI): Joined via Supermaven acquisition (Nov 2024).
- **Travis McPeak** (former CEO of cybersecurity startup Resourcely): Hired to lead security team.

### Broader Market Dynamics
- OpenAI responded to talent pressure with up to $1.5M retention bonuses and accelerated vesting.
- Principal AI researchers commanding $1M+/year packages; superstar individuals negotiating tens of millions over multi-year deals.
- Employment among software developers aged 22–25 fell ~20% between 2022 and 2025 (Stanford study) as AI tools displace entry-level coding work.

## Market Context

- AI coding tools market reached **$4–5B in total funding in 2025**; projected $12–15B by 2027.
- **GitHub Copilot** holds ~42% market share of paid AI coding tools; **Cursor** ~18% (up from near-zero 18 months prior).
- **Sequoia** backed at least four AI coding companies: Magic.dev, Sourcegraph, Factory AI, Zed.
- **YC** backed ~30 of 80 funded AI code assistant startups — most active early-stage pipeline in the market.
- **Y Combinator Winter 2025 batch:** 25% of startups generated 95% of their code using AI tools.
- **OpenAI Codex** (powered by codex-1, specialized o3 variant): 1M+ weekly active users as of Oct 2025 GA.
- Cursor hit $1B ARR in ~17 months — reportedly the fastest B2B SaaS to reach that milestone in history.
