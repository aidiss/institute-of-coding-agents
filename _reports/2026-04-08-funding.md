## Acquisitions & Exits

### OpenAI acquires Astral (Confirmed, March 19 2026)
OpenAI announced the acquisition of Astral, the company behind the Python developer tools **uv**, **Ruff**, and **ty**. These tools collectively reach hundreds of millions of downloads per month. The Astral team, led by founder **Charlie Marsh**, will join OpenAI's **Codex** team. OpenAI plans to integrate Astral's tooling into Codex to support the full development workflow — planning, code modification, tool execution, and maintenance. OpenAI committed to continuing open-source support post-close. Deal is subject to regulatory approval.
- Source: [OpenAI announcement](https://openai.com/index/openai-to-acquire-astral/) / [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/openai-to-acquire-developer-tooling-startup-astral.html)

### Anthropic acquires Bun (Confirmed, December 2025)
Anthropic's first-ever acquisition. Bun — the JavaScript runtime with 7M+ monthly downloads and 82K+ GitHub stars — was acquired to power Claude Code's infrastructure. The deal was announced alongside Claude Code reaching $1B ARR. Bun creator **Jarred Sumner** joined Anthropic. A notable side effect: in March 2026, Claude Code accidentally leaked its full source code because Bun's source maps were enabled in production builds.
- Source: [Anthropic blog](https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-acquires-bun-as-claude-code-reaches-usd1b-milestone) / [Bun blog](https://bun.com/blog/bun-joins-anthropic)

### Google acquires Windsurf (Confirmed, ~July 2025)
Google's $2.4B deal brought Windsurf CEO **Varun Mohan** and ~40 engineers to Google DeepMind, giving Google an established product team with both product intuition and technical depth in agentic coding right as the category broke out.

### Meta acqui-hires Dreamer AI team (Confirmed, March 2026)
Meta hired the full founding team of agentic AI startup **Dreamer** — including former Google and Stripe executives — into its **Superintelligence Labs** division. This was Meta's third major AI talent acquisition in four months.
- Source: [SiliconANGLE](https://siliconangle.com/2026/03/23/meta-acqui-hires-co-founders-agentic-ai-startup-dreamer/)

### Nvidia–Groq licensing deal / acqui-hire (Confirmed, 2026)
Nvidia paid **$20B** in a non-exclusive license for Groq's LPU (Language Processing Unit) inference chip architecture. Alongside the deal, Nvidia hired Groq founder/CEO **Jonathan Ross**, president **Sunny Madra**, and the majority of Groq's engineering team — effectively a hybrid acquisition.
- Source: [Techi.com](https://www.techi.com/nvidia-groq-deal-acqui-hire-ai-inference/)

## New Startups

**Entire** (Feb 2026) — Founded by former GitHub CEO **Thomas Dohmke**. Thesis: most code will be written by AI agents; developers need tooling for AI-generated code provenance and context. First product: **Checkpoints**, which captures the prompts, reasoning, and decisions made by AI agents as they write code. Raised $60M seed at $300M valuation.

**Sycamore** (announced Mar 2026) — Enterprise AI agent startup. Raised $65M seed (one of the largest seed rounds in the space) led by Coatue and Lightspeed, with angels including former OpenAI chief scientist **Bob McGrew** and Intel CEO **Lip-Bu Tan**.
