## Acquisitions & Exits

### OpenAI acquires Astral — Confirmed (Mar 19, 2026)
OpenAI acquired **Astral**, makers of the dominant open-source Python developer tooling: **uv** (package manager), **ruff** (linter/formatter), and **ty** (type checker), all Rust-based. The Astral team joins OpenAI's Codex division; deeper integration with Codex planned. OpenAI committed to maintaining the open-source products. This is OpenAI's ~6th acquisition in 2026, approaching its full-year 2025 total of 8.

### OpenAI acquires Promptfoo — Confirmed (Mar 9, 2026)
OpenAI acquired **Promptfoo**, an LLM security and red-teaming platform. Founded 2024, previously raised $23M at $86M valuation; used by 25%+ of Fortune 500. Will integrate into **OpenAI Frontier** (enterprise AI coworker). Open-source product continues.

### Windsurf (Codeium) — Final resolution (context from 2025)
After the OpenAI ~$3B deal fell through (Jul 2025), Google struck a licensing/acqui-hire for ~$2.4B, taking CEO Varun Mohan, co-founder Douglas Chen, and ~40 employees. **Cognition AI (Devin)** separately acquired the remaining Windsurf IP and product for ~$250M (Dec 2025), absorbing remaining staff. Last disclosed funding: $150M Series C at $1.25B valuation (General Catalyst, Aug 2024).
