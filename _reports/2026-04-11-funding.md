## Acquisitions & Exits

### Windsurf (Codeium) — complex two-part exit

Windsurf was previously Codeium, which had raised ~$243M total (last: $150M Series C at $1.25B valuation, General Catalyst, Aug 2024) before rebranding in April 2025.

1. **Google licensing deal (Jul 2025):** Google agreed to pay ~$2.4B to hire CEO Varun Mohan, co-founder Douglas Chen, and ~40 employees, and to license certain Windsurf IP. *Confirmed.*
2. **Cognition acquires remaining entity (Dec 2025):** After the Google deal left Windsurf as a headless company, interim CEO Jeff Wang led a sale of Windsurf's remaining IP, product, and staff to Cognition AI for ~$250M. The Windsurf brand continues under Cognition; Devin's autonomous agent capabilities are being integrated into the IDE. *Confirmed.*

### Anthropic acquires Bun (Dec 2025)

Anthropic made its first acquisition, purchasing Bun — a high-performance JavaScript runtime and developer toolkit with 7M monthly downloads. Strategic rationale: deepen developer tooling stack. *Confirmed.*

### SpaceX acquires xAI (Feb 2026)

All-stock merger valuing the combined entity at $1.25T (SpaceX $1T + xAI $250B). Largest corporate merger by valuation in history. Adjacent to coding-agent space through xAI's Grok coding capabilities. *Confirmed.*

## Market Context

Coding and developer-tool startups (Cognition, Poolside, Replit, Magic, Augment, Codeium/Windsurf, Factory, StackBlitz) collectively raised **>$3B**, making software development the highest-funded AI sub-category in 2025–2026.

Average round size among agentic AI startups that closed in Q4 2025 / early 2026: **$155M** — nearly double the $82M average for H1 2025 rounds.
