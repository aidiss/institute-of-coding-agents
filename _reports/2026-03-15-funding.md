### Acquisitions & Exits

**Cognition AI acquires Windsurf — ~$250M (Confirmed, July 2025)**
- Windsurf (formerly Codeium) went through three potential buyers in quick succession:
  1. **OpenAI** ($3B offer, collapsed over Microsoft/GitHub Copilot IP concerns)
  2. **Google** acqui-hired co-founders Varun Mohan, Douglas Chen, and ~40 engineers via a **$2.4B licensing deal**
  3. **Cognition** acquired the remaining IP, product, brand, and ~210 employees for **$250M**
- At acquisition, Windsurf had $82M ARR (down from $100M peak). Windsurf now owned by Cognition; enterprise clients include Goldman Sachs, Citi, Dell, Cisco, Palantir.
- **Status: Confirmed.**

**Anysphere acquires Graphite — ~$290M (Confirmed, December 2025)**
- Cursor's parent acquired the code-review platform Graphite shortly after its $52M Series B.
- **Status: Confirmed.**

**Meta acquires Manus AI — >$2B (Rumored/Reported)**
- Meta reportedly acquired AI agent startup **Manus**, which had reached ~$100M in ARR.
- **Status: Reported; not officially confirmed by Meta.**

**OpenAI acquires "io" — $6.5B (Confirmed)**
- OpenAI acquired **io**, a one-year-old hardware-software startup founded by former Apple design chief **Jony Ive**. No product had shipped; deal is a talent and hardware-integration play.
- **Status: Confirmed.**
