### Acquisitions & Exits

**Cognition AI acquires Windsurf (IP + product + ~210 employees) — $250M**
- Windsurf (formerly Codeium) had an extraordinary sequence of three potential acquirers in three months:
  1. **OpenAI** agreed to a **$3B acquisition** in May 2025, which collapsed after Microsoft's partnership terms would have given GitHub Copilot access to Windsurf's IP; Windsurf's CEO refused.
  2. **Google** hired Windsurf co-founders **Varun Mohan** and **Douglas Chen** plus ~40 employees via a **$2.4B licensing deal** (split between investors and employee compensation). Google got the talent; the company lost leadership.
  3. **Cognition AI** (makers of Devin) acquired the remaining IP, product, brand, and ~210 employees for **$250M** within 72 hours of Google's announcement.
- At acquisition, Windsurf had $82M ARR (down from $100M peak in April 2025, after Anthropic cut model access amid the OpenAI acquisition rumors).
- **Status: Confirmed.**

**Anysphere acquires Graphite — ~$290M (December 2025)**
- Cursor's maker acquired Graphite, a code review platform, shortly after Graphite closed a $52M Series B in 2025.
- **Status: Confirmed.**
