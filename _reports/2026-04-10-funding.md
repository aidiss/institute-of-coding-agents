## Acquisitions & Exits

### OpenAI acquires Astral — Confirmed (Mar 19, 2026)
OpenAI announced the acquisition of **Astral**, maker of Python developer tools **uv**, **Ruff**, and **ty** (hundreds of millions of downloads/month). Founder **Charlie Marsh** and the full Astral team join OpenAI's **Codex** team. Open-source support continues post-close. Terms undisclosed; subject to regulatory approval.

### OpenAI acquires Promptfoo — Confirmed (Mar 9, 2026)
OpenAI acquired **Promptfoo**, an AI security and red-teaming platform used by ~25% of Fortune 500 companies. Founders **Ian Webster** and **Michael D'Angelo** join OpenAI. Promptfoo's adversarial testing capabilities will be integrated into OpenAI **Frontier** for AI agent security. Promptfoo remains open source. Terms undisclosed.

### Meta acqui-hires Dreamer AI — Confirmed (Mar 23–24, 2026)
Meta's **Superintelligence Labs** (under Chief AI Officer Alexandr Wang) acqui-hired all three co-founders of agentic AI startup **Dreamer**: **David Singleton** (former Stripe CTO, Google Android VP), **Hugo Barra** (former Meta VR lead, Xiaomi), and **Nicholas Jitkoff** (former Google Chrome OS design). Dreamer had raised $56M at a $500M valuation (Nov 2024). Meta obtained a non-exclusive technology license; Dreamer remains a standalone legal entity. Terms undisclosed. Meta's third major AI talent acquisition in four months.

### OpenAI acqui-hires OpenClaw / Peter Steinberger — Confirmed (Feb 14–15, 2026)
OpenAI acquired open-source AI coding agent **OpenClaw** and its creator **Peter Steinberger**, who joined the **Codex** team. OpenClaw moves to a non-profit foundation and remains open source. Sam Altman described Steinberger as "a genius with a lot of amazing ideas about the future of very smart agents interacting with each other."

### Anthropic acquires Bun — Confirmed (Dec 2025)
Anthropic's first acquisition. **Bun** (JavaScript runtime; 7M+ monthly downloads, 82K+ GitHub stars) acquired to power Claude Code's execution layer. Creator **Jarred Sumner** joined Anthropic.

### Cognition acquires Windsurf — Confirmed (Jul 2025)
After Google poached Windsurf CEO **Varun Mohan** and ~40 engineers, Cognition acquired the remaining Windsurf team and technology. Windsurf brought $82M ARR and 350+ enterprise customers. Combined Cognition ARR more than doubled post-acquisition.

### Nvidia–Groq licensing/acqui-hire — Confirmed (late 2025/2026)
Nvidia paid **$20B** for a non-exclusive license to Groq's LPU (Language Processing Unit) inference architecture, simultaneously hiring Groq founder/CEO **Jonathan Ross**, president **Sunny Madra**, and the majority of Groq's engineering team.

**Regulatory note:** FTC Chair Andrew Ferguson announced in January 2026 the agency would investigate acqui-hires across the tech industry, citing antitrust concerns. OpenAI has already closed ~6 acquisitions in 2026 alone, approaching its full-year 2025 total.

## New Startups

**Entire** (Feb 2026) — Founded by former GitHub CEO **Thomas Dohmke**. Thesis: most code will be written by AI agents; developers need tooling to track provenance and context. First product: **Checkpoints**, capturing prompts, reasoning, and decisions behind AI-written code. $60M seed at $300M led by Felicis, with Madrona, Microsoft M12, and Basis Set.

**Sycamore** (Mar 2026) — Enterprise AI agent startup. Raised $65M seed (among the largest on record for the stage) co-led by Coatue and Lightspeed. Notable angels: former OpenAI chief scientist **Bob McGrew** and Intel CEO **Lip-Bu Tan**.

## Market Context

**Q1 2026 venture records:** Investors poured ~$300B into ~6,000 startups globally in Q1 2026, up 150%+ YoY. AI captured $242B — approximately 80% of total global VC in the quarter. Coding AI companies held 7 of the top 20 single-quarter financing rounds.

**Lovable M&A ambitions:** At $6.6B valuation and $400M ARR, Lovable CEO Anton Osika publicly announced the company is actively hunting acquisitions. Previous buy: cloud provider Molnett (Nov 2025). No new deal confirmed as of April 10.

**AI seed valuations rising:** Per TechCrunch analysis (Mar 31, 2026), "agentic" labeled startups are raising seed rounds at ~40% higher valuations than "Generative AI" labeled peers. Median pre-money seed valuation for AI companies hit a record high in Q1 2026.

**Codex market share signal:** JetBrains developer survey (week of Apr 3–9, 2026) shows Claude Code has risen to share second place alongside GitHub Copilot, each used by 18% of developers in professional settings. Cursor/Anysphere remains the overall leader.
