## Acquisitions & Exits

### Cognition acquires Windsurf (~$250M, July 14, 2025) — Confirmed
Cognition (maker of Devin) acquired the remaining assets of Windsurf (formerly Codeium) for an estimated **~$250M** after a chaotic 72-hour window in which:
1. An agreed **$3B OpenAI acquisition collapsed** (May–July 2025) due to Microsoft's contractual rights over OpenAI acquisitions.
2. Google executed a **$2.4B "reverse acquihire"** (July 11, 2025), hiring CEO Varun Mohan, co-founder Douglas Chen, and ~40 senior R&D staff into DeepMind's Gemini coding initiatives, while licensing Windsurf's technology non-exclusively.
3. Cognition moved within 72 hours to acquire the remaining IP, brand, product, and ~210 employees for ~$250M.

Windsurf had **$82M ARR** and 350+ enterprise clients (JPMorgan Chase, Dell) at time of acquisition. Post-acquisition, combined Cognition + Windsurf enterprise ARR grew 30%+ in the first seven weeks. As of February 2026, Windsurf ranked **#1** in the LogRocket AI Dev Tool Power Rankings.

### Meta acquires Manus AI (2026) — Confirmed
Meta acquired AI agent startup **Manus** for reportedly over **$2B**. Manus had approximately **$100M in annual recurring revenue** at the time of acquisition.

## Talent Moves

### Thomas Dohmke — GitHub CEO → founder of Entire (Feb 2026)
Former GitHub CEO **Thomas Dohmke** (left GitHub August 2025 after four years overseeing the rise of GitHub Copilot) launched **Entire**, an open-source developer platform for teams managing AI agent fleets. Raised $60M seed at $300M valuation. Backers include Jerry Yang (Yahoo co-founder), Garry Tan (YC CEO), Datadog CEO Olivier Pomel, and developer voices Gergely Orosz and Theo Browne.

### Google acquihires Windsurf founders (July 2025)
**Varun Mohan** (CEO) and **Douglas Chen** (co-founder) of Windsurf joined Google DeepMind's Gemini coding team as part of the $2.4B licensing/acquihire deal. ~40 senior R&D staff moved with them.

### Google acquihires Hume AI leadership (Jan 2026)
Google DeepMind hired **Alan Cowen** (CEO) and ~7 engineers from voice AI startup Hume AI to improve Gemini's voice features. **Andrew Ettinger** stepped in as Hume AI's new CEO.

## Market Context

- The global AI agent market grew from $5.25B (2024) to an estimated $7.84B (2025), with projections of $52.62B by 2030.
- ~85% of developers regularly use AI coding tools as of end-2025.
- U.S. AI engineer job postings rose 143% YoY in 2025; LinkedIn ranked the role as the #1 fastest-growing job title in 2026.
- OpenAI is reportedly building a GitHub competitor (reported by *The Information*, March 3, 2026), driven by frustration with service disruptions and the desire to own the full developer stack.
