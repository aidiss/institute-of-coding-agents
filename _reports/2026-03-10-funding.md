## Acquisitions & Exits

### Cognition acquires Windsurf (~$250M, July 14, 2025) — Confirmed
After OpenAI's **$3B acquisition collapsed** (May–July 2025) due to Microsoft's IP rights entanglement, and Google executed a **$2.4B reverse acquihire** of Windsurf's leadership (July 11), Cognition moved within 72 hours to acquire the remaining IP, brand, product, and ~210 employees. Windsurf had ~$82M ARR and 350+ enterprise clients (JPMorgan Chase, Dell) at acquisition. Post-deal, combined Cognition + Windsurf enterprise ARR grew 30%+ in seven weeks.

### Meta acquires Manus AI (2026) — Confirmed
Meta acquired AI agent startup **Manus** for a reported **$2B+**. Manus had approximately **$100M ARR** at time of acquisition.

### OpenAI acquires Windsurf — Collapsed (Rumored → Failed)
OpenAI's $3B definitive agreement for Windsurf was widely reported in April 2025 but ultimately fell apart; Microsoft's contractual IP rights over OpenAI acquisitions were the key obstacle. OpenAI has since pivoted to building its own developer-facing IDE and a reported **GitHub competitor** (per *The Information*, March 3, 2026).

## Talent Moves

### Thomas Dohmke — GitHub CEO → Entire founder (Feb 2026)
Former GitHub CEO **Thomas Dohmke** (departed GitHub August 2025 after overseeing GitHub Copilot's rise) launched **Entire**, an open-source platform for engineering teams managing AI agent fleets. The $60M seed is reportedly the largest-ever for a dev tool startup. Notable backers include Jerry Yang (Yahoo co-founder), Garry Tan (YC CEO), and Datadog CEO Olivier Pomel.

### Varun Mohan & Douglas Chen — Windsurf → Google DeepMind (July 2025)
**Varun Mohan** (CEO) and **Douglas Chen** (co-founder) of Windsurf joined Google DeepMind's Gemini coding team as part of the $2.4B licensing/acquihire deal. Approximately **40 senior R&D staff** moved with them, stripping Windsurf of most of its technical leadership before Cognition stepped in.

### Alan Cowen — Hume AI → Google DeepMind (Jan 2026)
Google DeepMind hired **Alan Cowen** (CEO) and ~7 engineers from voice AI startup **Hume AI** to strengthen Gemini's voice capabilities. **Andrew Ettinger** replaced Cowen as Hume AI's CEO.

## Market Context

- **U.S. AI investment:** ~$109.1B in private AI funding, nearly 12× China ($9.3B) and 24× the UK ($4.5B).
- **Mega-round concentration:** Nearly 20 U.S. AI startups raised $100M+ in the first six weeks of 2026 alone.
- **Developer adoption:** ~85% of developers regularly use AI coding tools as of end-2025; U.S. AI engineer postings rose 143% YoY.
- **Predicted 2026 acquisition targets (per investor commentary):** Factory, Codegen, Warp, Datadog, Sentry — observability and reliability tooling increasingly seen as strategic complements to foundation model providers.
- **"Vibe coding" mainstreaming:** Replit, Bolt.new, and Lovable (valued at $6.6B in Europe) are the primary beneficiaries of the natural-language-to-app wave.
