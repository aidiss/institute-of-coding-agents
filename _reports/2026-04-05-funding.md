## Acquisitions & Exits

### Cognition acquires Windsurf (July 2025, ~$250M — confirmed)
Cognition, maker of autonomous coding agent Devin, signed a definitive agreement to acquire Windsurf (formerly Codeium's IDE product). The deal included:
- Windsurf's IP, product, trademark, and brand
- **$82M ARR** and a business with enterprise ARR doubling quarter-over-quarter
- 350+ enterprise customers and hundreds of thousands of daily active users
- 100% of Windsurf employees with vesting cliffs waived

**Context:** Windsurf's earlier planned acquisition had collapsed after key leadership departed, leaving employees without expected payouts. Google had signed a licensing deal with Windsurf prior to Cognition stepping in. Cognition plans to merge Windsurf IDE capabilities with Devin for fully autonomous development workflows.

### Market context
The AI coding tools M&A market is expected to consolidate further in 2026. AI foundation models for code (primarily Poolside and Magic) accounted for $3.09B across 7 deals in the broader Q1 2026 VC activity summary.
