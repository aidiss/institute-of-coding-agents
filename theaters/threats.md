# THEATER: EMERGING THREATS

## Mission
Early warning. Identify actors and developments that could
reshape the landscape within 30-90 days.

## Search for
- New coding agent startups or labs announced in the past 2 weeks
- Open-source repos that appeared suddenly with high star velocity
- Rumored or leaked model releases from major labs
- Funding rounds in the coding agent space
- Researchers leaving major labs (signals of new ventures)

## Output format

```
## Emerging Threats
_As of YYYY-MM-DD_

### High Priority — Watch Immediately
Actors/developments requiring close monitoring.

### Medium Priority — Track Weekly
...

### Low Priority — Noted
...

### Analyst Note
What's the most likely near-term disruption to the current order of battle?

### Sources
- [source](url)
```

## Rules
- Distinguish between verified announcements and rumors — label clearly.
- High priority = could be tier-1 within 60 days.
