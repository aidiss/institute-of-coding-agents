## Acquisitions & Exits

### OpenAI acquires Astral (Confirmed, March 19 2026)
OpenAI announced the acquisition of Astral, maker of Python developer tools **uv**, **Ruff**, and **ty**, which collectively reach hundreds of millions of downloads per month. Founder **Charlie Marsh** and the Astral team join OpenAI's **Codex** team. OpenAI plans to integrate Astral tooling across Codex's full development workflow (planning, code modification, tool execution, maintenance). Open-source support will continue post-close. Subject to regulatory approval.
- Source: [OpenAI announcement](https://openai.com/index/openai-to-acquire-astral/) / [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/openai-to-acquire-developer-tooling-startup-astral.html)

### Anthropic acquires Bun (Confirmed, December 2025)
Anthropic's first acquisition. Bun — the JavaScript runtime with 7M+ monthly downloads and 82K+ GitHub stars — was acquired to power Claude Code's execution layer. Deal announced alongside Claude Code reaching $1B ARR. Bun creator **Jarred Sumner** joined Anthropic. Notable side-effect: in March 2026, Claude Code accidentally leaked its source code when Bun's source maps were left enabled in production builds.
- Source: [Anthropic blog](https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-acquires-bun-as-claude-code-reaches-usd1b-milestone) / [Bun blog](https://bun.com/blog/bun-joins-anthropic)

### Cognition acquires Windsurf (Confirmed, July 2025)
After Google poached Windsurf's CEO and ~40 engineers, Cognition acquired the remaining Windsurf team and technology for an undisclosed sum. Windsurf brought $82M ARR and 350+ enterprise customers. Combined ARR more than doubled post-acquisition; enterprise ARR up 30%+.
- Source: [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2025/07/14/cognition-maker-of-the-ai-coding-agent-devin-acquires-windsurf/) / [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/programming-development/remaining-windsurf-team-and-tech-acquired-by-cognition-makers-of-devin-were-friends-with-anthropic-again)

### Google acquires Windsurf leadership (Confirmed, ~July 2025)
Google's $2.4B deal brought Windsurf CEO **Varun Mohan** and ~40 engineers to Google DeepMind, directly before the remainder of Windsurf was acquired by Cognition.

### Meta acqui-hires Dreamer AI team (Confirmed, March 24 2026)
Meta hired the full founding team of agentic AI startup **Dreamer** — including former Google and Stripe executives — into its **Superintelligence Labs** division. Dreamer's agents focus on personal productivity tasks (email, scheduling). Meta's third major AI talent acquisition in four months.
- Source: [SiliconANGLE](https://siliconangle.com/2026/03/23/meta-acqui-hires-co-founders-agentic-ai-startup-dreamer/)

### OpenClaw acqui-hire by OpenAI (Confirmed, February 2026)
OpenAI acquired open-source AI coding agent **OpenClaw** and its creator **Peter Steinberger**, who joined the **Codex** team. OpenClaw had been the fastest-growing open-source AI coding agent at the time.
- Source: [Medium / Kurt Hamm](https://medium.com/@kurthamm/what-the-openclaw-acqui-hire-tells-us-about-the-agent-era-4abf4a8e13db)

### Nvidia–Groq licensing deal / acqui-hire (Confirmed, 2026)
Nvidia paid **$20B** for a non-exclusive license to Groq's LPU (Language Processing Unit) inference architecture, and simultaneously hired Groq founder/CEO **Jonathan Ross**, president **Sunny Madra**, and the majority of Groq's engineering team.
- Source: [Techi.com](https://www.techi.com/nvidia-groq-deal-acqui-hire-ai-inference/)

**Regulatory note:** FTC Chair Andrew Ferguson announced in January 2026 that the agency will investigate acqui-hires across the tech industry, citing antitrust concerns.

## New Startups

**Entire** (Feb 2026) — Founded by former GitHub CEO **Thomas Dohmke**. Thesis: most code will be written by AI agents; developers need tooling for provenance and context of AI-generated code. First product: **Checkpoints**, capturing the prompts, reasoning, and decisions behind AI-written code. Raised $60M seed at $300M valuation led by Felicis, with Madrona, Microsoft M12, and Basis Set.

**Sycamore** (Mar 2026) — Enterprise AI agent startup. Raised a $65M seed (among the largest on record for the stage) co-led by Coatue and Lightspeed. Notable angels: former OpenAI chief scientist **Bob McGrew** and Intel CEO **Lip-Bu Tan**.
