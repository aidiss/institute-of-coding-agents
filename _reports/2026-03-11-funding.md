## Acquisitions & Exits

### Cognition acquires Windsurf (~$250M, July 14, 2025) — Confirmed
After OpenAI's **$3B acquisition collapsed** (May–July 2025) due to Microsoft IP entanglement, and Google executed a **$2.4B reverse acquihire** of Windsurf leadership, Cognition acquired the remaining IP, brand, product, and ~210 employees within 72 hours. Windsurf had ~$82M ARR at acquisition; post-deal combined enterprise ARR grew 30%+ in seven weeks. Windsurf Wave 13 (early 2026) shipped multi-agent sessions, Git worktrees, and SWE-grep.

### Meta acquires Manus AI (2026) — Confirmed
Meta acquired AI agent startup **Manus** for a reported **$2B+**. Manus had approximately $100M ARR at time of acquisition.

### OpenAI acquires Windsurf — Collapsed (Failed)
OpenAI's $3B definitive agreement for Windsurf fell apart; Microsoft's contractual IP rights were the key obstacle. OpenAI has since pivoted to building its own developer-facing IDE and a reported **GitHub competitor** (*The Information*, Mar 3, 2026).

### Predicted targets (per investor commentary)
Factory, Codegen, Warp, Datadog, and Sentry are cited as likely M&A targets — AI observability and reliability tooling increasingly viewed as strategic complements for foundation model providers. **Datadog** has already invested in four agent observability players (LangChain, Arize, Braintrust, Patronus AI) since 2024. *The Information* published a 2026 prediction that **Microsoft will acquire an AI coding startup**.

## Talent Moves

### Thomas Dohmke — GitHub CEO → Entire founder (Feb 2026)
Former GitHub CEO **Thomas Dohmke** launched **Entire**, an open-source platform for engineering teams managing AI agent fleets. The $60M seed is reportedly the largest-ever for a dev tool startup. Backers include Jerry Yang (Yahoo co-founder), Garry Tan (YC CEO), and Datadog CEO Olivier Pomel.

### Varun Mohan & Douglas Chen — Windsurf → Google DeepMind (Jul 2025)
**Varun Mohan** (CEO) and **Douglas Chen** (co-founder) joined Google DeepMind's Gemini coding team as part of the $2.4B licensing/acquihire. ~40 senior R&D staff moved with them.

### AWS executive shakeups (early 2026)
- **David Luan** (Head of AGI Lab / Agentic AI R&D) departed AWS to start a new venture.
- **Scott Rosecrans** (VP of AI Sales) joined OpenAI.
- **Jigar Thakkar** (ex-Microsoft Teams founding member) joined AWS as VP of Agentic AI for Business.
- **Chet Kapoor** (ex-DataStax CEO) joined AWS as VP of Cybersecurity Services and Observability.

### Salesforce "6-for-5" leadership rotation (2026)
Five senior executives departed — including **Adam Evans** (EVP/GM of AI) — while six AI-specialized leaders with backgrounds in autonomous systems and reasoning logic were hired. **Joe Inzerillo** tapped to lead Slack + Agentforce convergence. Salesforce framed the moves as transitioning from "visionary development" to the "operational era" of AI agents.

### Alan Cowen — Hume AI → Google DeepMind (Jan 2026)
Google DeepMind hired **Alan Cowen** (CEO) and ~7 engineers from voice AI startup **Hume AI**. **Andrew Ettinger** replaced Cowen as Hume AI CEO.

## Market Context

- **U.S. AI investment:** ~$109.1B in private AI funding in 2025 — nearly 12× China ($9.3B) and 24× the UK ($4.5B).
- **Mega-round pace:** Nearly 20 U.S. AI startups raised $100M+ in the first six weeks of 2026.
- **Developer adoption:** ~85% of developers regularly use AI coding tools (end-2025); U.S. AI engineer job postings rose 143% YoY. LinkedIn ranked AI Engineer as the #1 fastest-growing U.S. job title in 2026.
- **Tech layoffs:** March 2026 saw ~45,000 tech layoffs; over 9,200 attributed directly to AI/automation. Amazon accounted for ~52% of those cuts.
- **Pricing wars:** Windsurf Pro at $15/month vs. Cursor Pro at $20/month; GitHub Copilot Free tier now at 50 premium requests/month. Cost-per-token is increasingly debated as intensely as raw capability.
- **Vibe coding:** Replit, Bolt.new, and Lovable (valued at $6.6B in Europe) are the primary beneficiaries of the natural-language-to-app wave.
