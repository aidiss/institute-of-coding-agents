## Acquisitions & Exits

### Windsurf (Codeium) — Sequential Deal Collapse and Acquihire (Apr–Jul 2025)

The most consequential deal sequence of the cycle:

1. **Apr 2025:** OpenAI enters exclusive acquisition talks for Windsurf at ~$3B (Bloomberg, CNBC). Confirmed.
2. **May 2025:** Agreement reportedly reached at $3B. Confirmed.
3. **Jul 11, 2025:** OpenAI's exclusivity expires — deal collapses. Confirmed (Fortune).
4. **Jul 11, 2025:** Google announces **$2.4B reverse acquihire** — a nonexclusive technology license plus hire of CEO **Varun Mohan**, co-founder **Douglas Chen**, and ~40 senior staff into Google DeepMind / Gemini team. Mohan and Chen's combined personal payout estimated ~$1.2B (TechCrunch). Confirmed.
5. **Jul 14, 2025:** **Cognition acquires remaining Windsurf entity** — IP, product, brand, ~350 enterprise customers, $82M ARR — for an estimated $250M. All remaining employees received full vesting acceleration. Deal price not formally confirmed; two-source TechCrunch estimate.

### Anysphere acquires Supermaven (Oct/Nov 2024)

- Cursor acquired Supermaven, an AI autocomplete startup founded by Jacob Jackson (also created Tabnine).
- Supermaven had raised $12M (Bessemer, John Schulman, Denis Yarats).
- Terms undisclosed. Confirmed.

### OpenAI acquires Astral (Mar 19, 2026)

- OpenAI acquired Astral, maker of **uv** (Python package manager), **Ruff** (linter/formatter), and **ty** (type checker) — all widely used open-source tools.
- Astral team joins OpenAI's Codex team.
- OpenAI committed to continuing open-source maintenance of all Astral tools.
- Financial terms undisclosed. Confirmed (CNBC, Astral blog).

### Poolside acquires Fern Labs (Nov 2025)

- Fern Labs specialized in forward-deployed agentic systems for enterprise/government.
- Intended to accelerate Poolside's "Bridge" forward-deployment program. Confirmed.

### Sourcegraph pivots from Cody to Amp (mid-2025)

- Sourcegraph deprecated Cody Free, Cody Pro, and Enterprise Starter plans (effective Jul 23, 2025).
- Launched **Amp** as its replacement AI coding agent product.
- Laid off ~24 employees (8% of staff). As of Mar 2026, ~190 employees. Confirmed.

## Talent Moves

### Remaining Windsurf staff → Cognition (Jul 2025)

### OpenAI departures — notable founder moves
