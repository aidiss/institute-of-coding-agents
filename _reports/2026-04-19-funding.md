### Regulatory Note

FTC Chair Andrew Ferguson opened a formal investigation into tech-sector acqui-hires in January 2026, flagging concern that the structure bypasses traditional antitrust review. The Google–Windsurf and Nvidia–Groq structures are among those under scrutiny.
